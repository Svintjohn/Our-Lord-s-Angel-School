Welcome to Our Lord's Angel School, a place where learning is guided by love, faith, and excellence. We believe that every child is a gift, capable of greatness when nurtured with care and purpose.

At Our Lord's Angel School, we offer a well-rounded education grounded in Christian values and academic integrity. Our dedicated teachers and staff work together to create a safe, ,inspiring, and respectful environment where students grow intellectually, emotionally, and spiritually. From early childhood to elementary levels, we emphasize discipline, compassion, and a passion for learning. With modern facilities, supportive programs, and a strong sense of community, we help students build character, confidence, and a clear sense of purpose. Whether in the classroom, on the field, or through outreach, we aim to form responsible and faith-driven individuals.

We invite you to become part of the Our Lord's Angel School family—where hearts are shaped, minds are challenged, and lives are inspired. Together, let’s raise future leaders who will shine with wisdom and kindness.
